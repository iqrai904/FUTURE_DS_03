# FUTURE_DS_03
# Marketing Funnel & Conversion Analysis

## Overview

This project analyzes the effectiveness of a bank marketing campaign using the **Bank Marketing Dataset**. The analysis identifies customer segments with the highest conversion rates and provides actionable business recommendations to improve future marketing campaigns. Python was used for data cleaning and analysis, while Tableau was used to build an interactive dashboard for data visualization.

---

## Tools Used

- Python (Pandas, Matplotlib)
- Tableau Public
- Microsoft Excel
- Jupyter Notebook
- GitHub

---

## Objectives

- Clean and prepare the marketing campaign dataset.
- Analyze customer conversion behavior.
- Identify factors influencing customer conversions.
- Compare conversion rates across different customer segments.
- Build an interactive Tableau dashboard.
- Generate business recommendations based on the analysis.

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

| KPI | Value |
|------|------:|
| Total Customers | 45,211 |
| Converted Customers | 5,289 |
| Conversion Rate | 11.70% |
| Average Customer Age | 41.67 Years |
---

## Key Insights

- Overall Conversion Rate: **11.70%**
- Students recorded the highest conversion rate among all job categories.
- Customers aged **60+** showed the highest likelihood of conversion.
- Customers with tertiary education achieved the highest conversion rate.
- Cellular communication produced the best campaign results.
- March recorded the highest conversion rate, while May showed comparatively lower campaign effectiveness despite having a high number of campaign contacts.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Focus future marketing campaigns on high-converting customer segments such as students and retirees.
- Prioritize cellular communication to improve customer engagement.
- Schedule campaigns during high-performing months such as March, September, October, and December.
- Develop personalized marketing strategies based on customer demographics.
- Review campaign strategies used during lower-performing months to improve conversion rates.
